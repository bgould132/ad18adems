How to Vote

Important Voting Information

To vote in the AD18 Assembly District Election Meeting (ADEM), you must:

  1. Be a registered Democrat living in Assembly District 18
  2. Register with CADEM to receive your ballot and verify eligibility
Two Ways to Vote
1. Online Voting
  • Register by January 24, 2025 at 12:00 PM
  • Receive voting credentials by mail by January 31, 2025
  • Vote using the ElectionBuddy platform
2. In-Person Voting
  • Register by February 23, 2025 at 6:00 PM
  • Bring your identification code to the voting location
  • Fill out a paper ballot
Important: You can vote for up to 14 candidates. Ballots will be voided if they include more than 14 selections or have missing/incorrect identification codes.
Register to Vote

Frequently Asked Questions

ADEM elections select Assembly District delegates who help shape the California Democratic Party's platform, endorse candidates, and advocate for progressive policies. These delegates represent your local community in important party decisions.

You are eligible to vote if you are:
  • A registered Democrat
  • Living in Assembly District 18 (most of Oakland, Alameda, or Emeryville)
  • Registered to vote at your current address
Not sure if you're in AD18? You can check your district at findyourrep.legislature.ca.gov

For in-person voting, you'll need:
  • Your voter identification code (received after registration)
  • A valid form of identification
  • Proof of residence in AD18 (if your ID shows a different address)

To ensure your vote counts:
  • Vote for no more than 14 candidates
  • Include your correct voter identification code
  • Submit your ballot before the deadline
  • Follow all instructions carefully when filling out your ballot
If voting online, make sure to complete all steps in the ElectionBuddy platform.

Why Your Vote Matters

Shape Progressive Policy

ADEMs help shape the California Democratic Party platform through direct participation and voting. Your vote ensures the party champions meaningful solutions for housing affordability, climate action, and community needs while staying true to progressive values.

Advocate for Local Communities

Your ADEMs connect Assembly District 18's needs with state party resources and attention. From affordable housing to infrastructure improvements, we're committed to bringing progressive solutions to Alameda, Oakland, and Emeryville.

Support Progressive Champions

ADEMs play a crucial role in endorsing Democratic candidates. By electing our slate, you help ensure support for candidates who combine progressive values with practical experience in delivering positive change for our communities.

Strengthen Our Democratic Party

This is your opportunity to help build a more effective and responsive Democratic Party. We'll work to ensure the party stays true to its progressive roots while focusing on practical solutions to make real progress on housing, climate, and social justice.
